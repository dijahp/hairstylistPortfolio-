About Lida

Originally from and currently working out of Atlanta, Georgia, Lida is a licensed hairstylist specializing in balayage and highlighting.

From a young age she's been an artist in many different mediums ranging from pen and paper to jewelry making and more, with an especially strong eye for design.

Initially planning to pursue a career in the graphic arts, she eventually discovered that her true passion lay in hairdressing, and the rest, as they say, is history.

What sets Lida apart from her peers in the industry is her innate capability to transform clients, rather than just trying to cover them up.

She firmly believes that all people are beautiful, and she simply enjoys enhancing their natural gifts.
